Teaching

I teach a range of courses, including Chinese language courses and courses on second language acquisition and research methodology. Below are the courses I have taught:

  • Reading and Writing
    A language course designed to teach international students Chinese reading and writing skills.
  • Using R for Second Language Research
    This course introduces graduate students to data analysis using R for second language acquisition research.
  • Eye Tracking for Second Language Research
    A lecture within the course Research Methods in Second Language Acquisition, focusing on eye-tracking technology in second language research.
